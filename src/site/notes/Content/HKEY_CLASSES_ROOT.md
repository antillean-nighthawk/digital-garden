### Running shell commands
- `.\exefile\shell\open\command`
- `.\batfile\shell\open\command`
- `.\comfile\shell\open\command`
- `.\Drive\shell` or `.\Drive\shell\cmd\command`
- `.\Folder\shell` or `.\Folder\shell\cmd\command`
